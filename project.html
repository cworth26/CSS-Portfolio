For Project one, my group members and I thought it'd be really helpful for students to have a cool website that allowed them to look up daily definitions and have gifs (giphy images) pop up with the corresponding definition! Our user story stemmed from a school disctrict wanting a web application that allows you to search for words and have their definitions displayed with a fun and cool giphy image! For future improvement, we want to design the website to look like a motivational poster so that visually it's something that students will be interested in. We also would like to improve the enagement of the site it self and allow users to create login information, and give users the ability to comment on word and their definitions and to what their teacher and fellow classmates post. For Project two, my group members and I created a dashboard community for coders. Within this dashoard, students have the ability to track the hours they used to study throughout the week, chat with fellow coding students and even share resources! This project was challenging but our hard work paid off! We were able to successfully launch this app just in time for it's unveiling! For future development, we want to add LIVE chat feature that will send automated messages when people are offline. We also would like to include the ability to create videos through the app and post them for other coding students to see!
